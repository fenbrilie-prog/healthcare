FAQ

Questions your team will ask

The questions a healthcare GC, CIO, privacy officer, or procurement team raises before any new system goes in. If you have more, talk to our team.

Where does our data sit, and what certifications does Checkbox hold? +
Checkbox is SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 certified, and compliant with ISO 27017 and ISO 27018 frameworks. Our security team provides the full documentation package up front so your IT and information security teams can complete review in parallel with your evaluation.
How should we think about PHI and HIPAA? +
Checkbox is the legal service hub, not a clinical system. Healthcare teams typically design intake forms and workflows so PHI is referenced by matter number, not stored verbatim in Checkbox. Our security team will walk your privacy officer through the data flow, the certifications above, and what they need for their HIPAA risk analysis.
Is our data used to train AI models? +
No. Checkbox's AI features use large language models under a zero-retention agreement. Your data is not stored by the model provider and is not used to train external AI models. Our security documentation includes the technical detail your CIO and privacy officer will want to see.
We already have a CLM. Where does Checkbox fit? +
Checkbox sits in front of your CLM as the front door for legal. It captures the request, classifies it, and only contracts that need full CLM treatment get routed there. Checkbox integrates with Ironclad and Ivo so contract data flows cleanly between systems. If you don't have a CLM, most teams find Checkbox's contract intake sufficient for the volume they handle.
Our health system has multiple entities. Can Checkbox handle that? +
Yes. Custom fields, configurable views, and matter routing all support multi-entity environments. Teams typically configure entity as a field on every matter, then use views and dashboards to slice by entity, hospital, or affiliate. One system, full visibility across the parent and every subsidiary.
Our legal team is small (2-5 attorneys). Is this overkill? +
It's the opposite. Lean health system legal teams carry the highest request volume per attorney of any in-house environment, and the highest regulatory exposure. The Front Door is how a small team scales: AI handles the routine, structure handles the rest, and every hour saved on triage becomes capacity for matters that need real legal judgement.
Who builds and maintains the workflows: legal or IT? +
Legal. Checkbox is no-code. Your legal ops or paralegal team builds and updates workflows directly, without filing an IT ticket. Hitachi Digital's legal team went from 3 planned workflows to 11 in production in 3 months, with no IT dependency. That's the design pattern Checkbox is built around.
How fast can we get one workflow live? +
Weeks, not quarters. Most teams have their first intake workflow live within the first month, with security review running in parallel. Subsequent workflows take days. Talk to our team about a typical implementation path for a health system of your size.
